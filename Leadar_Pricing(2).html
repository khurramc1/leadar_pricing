Common Questions
Everything you need to know before getting started
What does "Leadar Platform included" actually mean? +
Every package includes Leadar Hosting, Leadar Domains, Leadar CRM, and Leadar Marketing Platform — these are our own tools, built and operated by us. You don't need HubSpot, Mailchimp, GoDaddy, or any other separate subscription. It's all on one invoice, managed by one team. Most agencies pass third-party tool costs on to you as extras; we don't.
Is there a minimum contract length? +
We run a 6-month pilot to allow enough time to see meaningful SEO results. After the pilot, all packages move to month-to-month with 30 days written notice. We earn your business every month — no 12-month lock-ins.
What is GEO / AI Search Visibility? +
GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is how we build your brand's presence inside AI-generated answers — in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. We track 15 specific prompts weekly using Otterly.ai and build content designed to earn citations in those answers. Available from the Accelerated package. Most agencies in Singapore are not offering this yet — it's a first-mover advantage.
Who owns the content, website, and accounts? +
You own everything — always. All content produced, your website, your Google accounts, your domain, your CRM data. If you ever leave, you take it all with you. We believe in earning your business month-to-month, not locking you in through asset ownership.
Why is Startup SGD 500/month and not cheaper? +
SGD 500/month is already significantly below market rate for what's included — a managed WordPress website, full platform onboarding (hosting, domain, CRM, email), keyword research, on-page SEO, and 2 articles per month. Below this price point it's not possible to deliver quality work that actually moves rankings. What we're offering at SGD 500 is a genuine foundation — not a placeholder service.
Can I upgrade packages mid-contract? +
Yes — you can upgrade at any point. Most clients start on Startup and move up once they've seen early results and built trust in the process. We'll scope the Accelerated or Growth package together based on what you need at that stage. Downgrades are available after the 6-month pilot on 30 days notice.
What happens after I click "Start with [Package]"? +
For Startup — clicking the button opens the Leadar proposal builder with the tier pre-selected. Fill in a brief about your business (about 10 minutes) and it generates a customised proposal. For Accelerated and Growth — clicking "Get in Touch" opens an email to us. We'll schedule a short discovery call to scope the right package for your goals. Onboarding begins within 48 hours of agreement.
